Mods

Allowed Mods

  • HUDs
  • Keybinds // Command Macros
  • Optifine // FPS Boosting Mods
  • Toggle Sneak/Sprint Mods // BetterSprint 1.8 is NOT allowed
  • Damage Indicators
  • Better PvP Mod // Auto-Pot, Macro for auto eat, quick-use, and Minimap (with player locations) should all be disabled
  • Brightness Mods
  • Replay Mod
  • Schematica and World Download Mods
  • 5zig mod or LabyMod
  • Liteloader
  • TCPNoDelay
  • Schematica mod is allowed for outlines only
  • Cape Mod

Non-Allowed Mods

  • Hacked Clients of any kind
  • Minimaps that show and track players, mobs, or underground caves/structures
  • Autoclickers, double clickers, etc.
  • Inventory Tweaks
  • Raid Alerts
  • X-Ray Texture Packs or Modifications
  • Fly Boost
  • Action Macros or Mods // Macros or mods that enhance player abilities against other players and give unfair advantages to yourself.
    Examples include auto-sell-hand-bots, spam bots, etc.
